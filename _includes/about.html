About

Originally from Australia, I received a PhD from the University of Sydney in 2015. I worked as a postdoc at the University of Geneva (Switzerland) from 2015 - 2018, and at the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy in Heidelberg (Germany) in early-2019. My research focused on high-resolution imaging of planetary systems, to discover and study extrasolar planets and brown dwarfs.

In late-2019 I participated in the Insight Data Science Fellowship, working on a website to classify jigsaw puzzle images and text reviews.

In 2020-2021 I worked as a Data Scientist in the digital media chapter of WooliesX, the digital arm of Woolworths (Australia's largest supermarket chain).

Since June 2021 I have worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Nielsen, working on Digital Ad Ratings for Connected TV clients.
